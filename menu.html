Classic Mocktails

Hugs on the Beach
Lemonade, peach syrup, orange juice, pineapple juice — Crisp, fruity, tropical
Instructions Add 20 ml peach syrup, 40 ml orange juice, 20 ml pineapple juice and 60 ml lemonade into a chilled glass over fresh ice and garnish with a lemon twist.
Kindapolitan (White)
Lime juice, elderflower cordial, orange juice — Zesty, floral, bright
Instructions Add 20 ml lime juice, 20 ml elderflower cordial and 50 ml orange juice into a glass. Garnish with a lime wedge.
Bahama Calma
Orange juice, pineapple juice, creamed coconut — Tropical, creamy, zesty
Instructions Soften 30 g creamed coconut by stirring it with 20 ml warm water until smooth. Add 120 ml orange juice and 60 ml pineapple juice with ice into a glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a pineapple wedge.
Mockerol Spritz
Crodino or Aperitivo, tonic water — Bitter, refreshing, Italian
Instructions Pour 100 ml Crodino or Aperitivo over ice in a wine glass, top with 100 ml tonic water, and garnish with an orange slice.
Piña No-lada
Creamed coconut, pineapple juice, vanilla — Creamy, tropical, sweet
Instructions Blend 30 g of creamed coconut with 150 ml pineapple juice and about 1/4 tsp vanilla extract until smooth. Serve over ice.
Nojito
Lime juice, dried mint, tonic water, elderflower cordial — Minty, cool, crisp
Instructions Rehydrate 1 tsp dried mint in 15 ml warm water for 5 minutes. Add 15 ml lime juice and 15 ml elderflower cordial, then top with 150 ml tonic water over ice. Stir gently.
Tequiless Sunrise
Orange juice, mango juice, sangria syrup — Bright, layered, citrusy
Instructions Pour 100 ml orange juice and 50 ml mango juice over ice. Slowly pour 10 ml sangria syrup to create a layered effect. Garnish with an orange slice.
Shan’ty
Impossibrew, lemonade, lime juice — Zingy, light, fizzy
Instructions Mix 100 ml Impossibrew, 100 ml lemonade and 10 ml lime juice over ice. Stir and serve.
Belli-no
Peach syrup, alcohol-free merlot — Fruity, bright, bubbly
Instructions Pour 15 ml peach syrup into a flute glass and top with 100 ml alcohol-free merlot.
Caipiri-nah
Lime juice, sugar, tonic water — Zesty, simple, Brazilian
Instructions Mix 1 tsp sugar with 30 ml lime juice in a glass. Add ice and top with 150 ml tonic water. Stir gently.
Espresso Marti-no
Espresso, sugar & water, vanilla extract, plant milk — Strong, sweet, creamy
Instructions Combine 60 ml cooled espresso, 30 g granulated sugar, 30 ml water, 1/4 tsp vanilla extract and 30 ml plant milk into a glass. Garnish with cocoa powder and coffee beans if desired.
Free & T
Tonic water, lime juice, elderflower cordial — Botanical, crisp, clean
Instructions Combine 150 ml tonic water, 10 ml lime juice, and 10 ml elderflower cordial over ice. Stir and serve.
Margaritish
Lime juice, lemon juice, orange juice, maple syrup, tonic water — Citrusy, tangy, spritzy
Instructions Add 20 ml lime juice, 10 ml lemon juice, 30 ml orange juice, and 10 ml maple syrup to a glass with ice. Stir well, then top with 150 ml tonic water.
Sans-gria
Sangria syrup, alcohol-free merlot, orange juice — Bright, floral, tangy
Instructions Stir 30 ml sangria syrup, 100 ml alcohol-free merlot, and 100 ml orange juice over ice. Serve with fruit garnish if desired.
Popstar Marti-no
Mango juice, lime juice, peach syrup, vanilla — Tropical, cheeky, fun
Instructions Shake or stir 50 ml mango juice, 10 ml lime juice, 15 ml peach syrup, and about 1/4 tsp vanilla extract with ice into a glass.
Mi-nosa
Orange juice, tonic water — Crisp, bitter, refreshing
Instructions Pour 60 ml orange juice into a flute or wine glass, then top with 60 ml chilled tonic water. Stir gently and garnish with an orange twist.
Young Island Iced Tea
English Breakfast tea, apple cider vinegar, ginger beer, lemon juice — Spicy, tangy, refreshing
Instructions Brew 1 English Breakfast tea bag in 75 ml boiling water for 5 minutes. Remove the bag and let the tea cool. Mix with 7.5 ml apple cider vinegar, 75 ml ginger beer, and 7.5 ml lemon juice over ice. Stir gently.

Shakes & Floats

Chocolate Milkshake
Vanilla ice cream, cocoa powder, plant milk — Rich, creamy, chocolatey
Instructions Place 120 g vanilla ice cream in your blender, add 7 g cocoa powder and 150 ml plant milk. Blend until smooth, then pour into a chilled glass. Top with whipped cream and a dusting of cocoa powder.
Chocolate Banana Swirl
Banana, cocoa powder, plant milk — Rich, smooth, indulgent
Instructions Blend 1 banana, 7 g cocoa powder and 150 ml plant milk until velvety smooth. Serve immediately.
Peanut Butter Milkshake
Vanilla ice cream, plant milk, peanut butter — Creamy, nutty, indulgent
Instructions Blend 120 g vanilla ice cream with 150 ml plant milk and 30 g peanut butter until smooth. Serve with a drizzle of peanut butter on top.
Hazelnut Milkshake
Nutella, vegan vanilla ice cream, plant milk — Creamy, chocolatey, nutty
Instructions Blend 60 g (≈2 Tbsp) Nutella with 120 g vegan vanilla ice cream and 150 ml barista-style plant milk until smooth. Pour into a chilled glass, then top with a swirl of Nutella or a sprinkle of chopped hazelnuts.
Banana Hazelnut Milkshake
Nutella, banana, vegan vanilla ice cream, plant milk — Creamy, chocolatey, fruity
Instructions Blend 60 g Nutella, 1 ripe banana, 120 g vegan vanilla ice cream and 150 ml barista-style plant milk until smooth. Pour into a chilled glass and garnish with a banana slice or a drizzle of Nutella.
Creamy Citrus Shake
Orange juice, double cream, vanilla extract, ice — Silky, citrus, dreamy
Instructions Blend 100 ml orange juice, 30 ml double cream, 1 ml vanilla extract and 100 g ice until smooth and frothy. Pour into a glass and serve cold.
Mocha Banana Blitz
Banana, cocoa powder, espresso, ice — Bold, creamy, energising
Instructions Brew a 30 ml shot of espresso and let it cool. Blend 1 banana, 2 g cocoa powder, 30 ml cooled espresso and 100 g ice until velvety. Serve immediately.
Peach Tea Cream Float
Peach iced tea, vanilla ice cream, whipped cream, ice — Creamy, fruity, fizzy
Instructions Fill a glass with 100 g ice, pour 150 ml peach iced tea over it, top with 60 g vanilla ice cream and swirl in 15 ml whipped cream.
Tropical Nice Cream
Pineapple juice, banana, ice — Frozen, fruity, light
Instructions Blend 100 ml pineapple juice, 1 banana and 100 g ice until thick and scoopable. Serve immediately.
Vanilla Cola Float
Coke Zero, vanilla ice cream — Classic, bubbly, rich
Instructions Place 60 g vanilla ice cream into a tall glass, then pour over 150 ml Coke Zero. Serve immediately.
Coconut Ice Swirl
Creamed coconut, ice, elderflower cordial — Chilled, tropical, lush
Instructions Blend 30 g creamed coconut with 100 g ice until smooth. Drizzle 10 ml elderflower cordial on top and serve.
Caramel Milkshake
Vanilla ice cream, plant milk, caramel flavour, ice — Creamy, sweet, indulgent
Instructions Blend 120 g vanilla ice cream, 150 ml plant milk, 15 ml caramel flavour and 100 g ice until smooth. Serve with optional whipped cream.
Peppermint Shake
Vanilla ice cream, plant milk, peppermint extract, ice — Cool, minty, fresh
Instructions Blend 120 g vanilla ice cream, 150 ml plant milk, 2 ml American peppermint extract and 100 g ice until creamy.

Americano

Instructions Press “Americano” on the main screen. The machine will brew espresso and add hot water.
Cappuccino
Espresso, foamed plant milk — Frothy, rich, warming
Instructions Press “Cappuccino” on the main screen, choose your froth level (1–3), then press start.
Latte

Instructions Press “Latte Macchiato” on the main screen. The machine will brew espresso and steam plant milk.
Macchiato

Instructions Press “Latte Macchiato” on the main screen, select foam level, then press start.
Cortado

Mocha
Espresso, cocoa powder, plant milk — Chocolaty, creamy, bold
Instructions Brew an espresso shot (press “Espresso”), stir in 1 tsp cocoa powder, then tap “More drinks” → “Caffè Latte” for steamed milk.
Mocha Cortado
Espresso, cocoa powder, steamed plant milk — Chocolatey, bold, comforting
Instructions Press “Espresso” to brew a shot, stir in 1 tsp cocoa powder, then tap “More drinks” → “Milk froth” and top with foam.
Peppermint Mocha
Espresso, cocoa powder, plant milk, peppermint extract — Minty, chocolatey, warming
Instructions Shake 50ml espresso, 1 tsp cocoa powder and 1ml peppermint extract with ice for 8s. Strain into a mug, then press “Cappuccino” (froth level 2) to add foamed milk. Stir once.
Caramel Latte
Espresso, plant milk, caramel flavour — Sweet, smooth, rich
Instructions Stir 10ml caramel flavour into a shot of espresso. Press “Latte” (milk volume 3) to add steamed milk.
