Classic Mocktails

Hugs on the Beach
Lemonade, peach syrup, lemon juice, orange juice, pineapple juice — Crisp, fruity, tropical
Instructions Place 20 ml peach syrup, 20 ml lemon juice, 40 ml orange juice, 20 ml pineapple juice and 40 ml soda lemonade into a large jug. Stir briskly in a jug with a long spoon for about 20 s. Pour into a chilled glass over fresh ice and garnish with a lemon twist.
White Kindapolitan
Lime juice, elderflower cordial, orange juice — Zesty, floral, bright
Instructions Shake 20ml lime juice, 20ml cordial and 50ml orange juice with ice. Strain and garnish.
Bahama Calma
Orange juice, pineapple juice, creamed coconut — Tropical, creamy, zesty
Instructions Soften 30 g creamed coconut by stirring it with 20 ml warm water until smooth. Combine with 120 ml orange juice and 60 ml pineapple juice in a lidded jar or container with ice. Seal and shake vigorously for 10 s, then strain into a chilled glass over fresh ice. Garnish with a pineapple wedge.
Piña No-lada
Creamed coconut, pineapple juice, vanilla — Creamy, tropical, sweet
Instructions Blend 2 tbsp creamed coconut with 150ml pineapple juice and a drop of vanilla extract until smooth.
Nojito
Lime juice, dried mint, tonic water, elderflower cordial — Minty, cool, crisp
Instructions Rehydrate 1 tsp dried mint in 15ml warm water for 5 min. Add 15ml lime juice and 15ml cordial, top with 150ml tonic water over ice.
Tequiless Sunrise
Orange juice, mango juice, sangria syrup — Bright, layered, citrusy
Instructions Pour 100ml orange juice and 50ml mango juice over ice. Layer 10ml syrup, garnish.
Shan’ty
Impossibrew, lemonade, lime juice — Zingy, light, fizzy
Instructions Mix 100ml Impossibrew, 100ml lemonade and 10ml lime juice over ice.
Belli-no
peach syrup, alcohol-free merlot — Fruity, bright, bubbly
Instructions Pour 15ml syrup and top with 100ml merlot in a flute.
Caipiri-nah
Lime juice, sugar, tonic water — Zesty, simple, Brazilian
Instructions Muddle sugar with 30ml lime juice, add 150ml tonic water over ice.
Espresso Marti-no
Espresso, sugar & water, vanilla extract, plant milk — Strong, sweet, creamy
Instructions Combine 60 ml cooled espresso, 30 g granulated sugar, 30 ml water, ¼ tsp vanilla extract and 30 ml barista-style plant milk in a container with ice. Stir vigorously for 10 s, until the sugar has dissolved, then strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with coffee beans.
Free & T
Tonic water, lime juice, elderflower cordial — Botanical, crisp, clean
Instructions Combine 150ml tonic water, 10ml lime juice, 10ml cordial over ice.
Margaritish
Lime juice, lemon juice, orange juice, maple syrup, tonic water — Citrusy, tangy, spritzy
Instructions Shake juices and syrup, strain over ice, top with 150ml tonic water.
Sans-gria
Sangria syrup, alcohol-free merlot, orange juice — Bright, floral, tangy
Instructions Stir 30ml sangria syrup, 100ml merlot, 100ml orange juice over ice.
Popstar Marti-no
Mango juice, lime juice, peach syrup, vanilla — Tropical, cheeky, fun
Instructions Shake juices, syrup and extract with ice.

Shakes & Floats

Chocolate Milkshake
Vanilla ice cream, cocoa powder, plant milk — Rich, creamy, chocolatey
Instructions Place 120 g vanilla ice cream in your blender, add 7 g cocoa powder and 150 ml plant milk. Blend until smooth, then pour into a chilled glass. Top with whipped cream and a dusting of cocoa powder.
Chocolate Banana Swirl
Banana, cocoa powder, plant milk — Rich, smooth, indulgent
Instructions Blend 1 banana, 7 g cocoa powder and 150 ml plant milk until velvety smooth. Serve immediately.
Creamy Citrus Shake
Orange juice, double cream, vanilla extract, ice — Silky, citrus, dreamy
Instructions Blend 100 ml orange juice, 30 ml double cream, 1 ml vanilla extract and 100 g ice until smooth and frothy. Pour into a glass and serve cold.
Mocha Banana Blitz
Banana, cocoa powder, espresso, ice — Bold, creamy, energising
Instructions Brew a 30 ml shot of espresso and let it cool. Blend 1 banana, 2 g cocoa powder, 30 ml cooled espresso and 100 g ice until velvety. Serve immediately.
Peach Tea Cream Float
Peach iced tea, vanilla ice cream, whipped cream, ice — Creamy, fruity, fizzy
Instructions Fill a glass with 100 g ice, pour 150 ml peach iced tea over it, top with 60 g vanilla ice cream and swirl in 15 ml whipped cream.
Tropical Nice Cream

Instructions Blend 100 ml pineapple juice, 1 banana and 100 g ice until thick and scoopable. Serve immediately.
Vanilla Cola Float

Instructions Place 60 g vanilla ice cream into a tall glass, then pour over 150 ml Coke Zero. Serve immediately.
Coconut Ice Swirl
Creamed coconut, ice, elderflower cordial — Chilled, tropical, lush
Instructions Blend 30 g creamed coconut with 100 g ice until smooth. Drizzle 10 ml elderflower cordial on top and serve.
Caramel Milkshake
Vanilla ice cream, plant milk, caramel flavour, ice — Creamy, sweet, indulgent
Instructions Blend 120 g vanilla ice cream, 150 ml plant milk, 15 ml caramel flavour and 100 g ice until smooth. Serve with optional whipped cream.
Peppermint Shake
Vanilla ice cream, plant milk, peppermint extract, ice — Cool, minty, fresh
Instructions Blend 120 g vanilla ice cream, 150 ml plant milk, 2 ml American peppermint extract and 100 g ice until creamy.
