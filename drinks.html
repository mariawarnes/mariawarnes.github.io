🍸 Classic Mocktails

🍊 Hugs on the Beach
Soda water, Monin White Peach syrup, lemon juice, orange juice, pineapple juice — Crisp, fruity, tropical
Instructions Place 20 ml peach syrup, 20 ml lemon juice, 40 ml orange juice, 20 ml pineapple juice and 40 ml soda water into a large lidded jar or container. Seal and roll or shake the container vigorously for 10–15 s; if you don’t have a lid, stir briskly in a jug with a long spoon for about 20 s. Pour into a chilled glass over fresh ice and garnish with a lemon twist.
🍋 White Kindapolitan
Lime juice, elderflower cordial, orange juice — Zesty, floral, bright
InstructionsShake 20ml lime juice, 20ml elderflower cordial and 50ml orange juice with ice for 8 seconds. Strain into a coupe and garnish with an orange twist.
🥥 Piña No-lada
Creamed coconut, pineapple juice, vanilla — Creamy, tropical, sweet
InstructionsBlend 2 tbsp creamed coconut with 150ml pineapple juice and a drop of vanilla extract until smooth. Pour into a glass over fresh ice and garnish with a pineapple wedge.
🍋 Nojito
Lime juice, mint, soda water, elderflower cordial — Minty, cool, crisp
InstructionsRehydrate 1 tsp of dried mint with 15ml warm water in the glass and let it sit for 5 minutes until it softens. Gently press the softened mint and then add 15ml lime juice and 15ml elderflower cordial. Add crushed ice, top with soda water, stir well and serve.
🍊 Tequiless Sunrise
Orange juice, mango juice, Monin Sangria syrup — Bright, layered, citrusy
InstructionsPour 100ml orange juice and 50ml mango juice into a tall glass with ice. Slowly pour 10ml Monin Sangria syrup over the back of a spoon. Garnish with an orange slice.
🍺 Shan’ty
Corona Cero, Schweppes lemonade, lime juice — Zingy, light, fizzy
InstructionsFill a glass with ice, add 100ml Corona Cero, 100ml Schweppes lemonade and 10ml lime juice. Stir gently and serve.
🍑 Belli-no
Monin White Peach syrup, alcohol-free merlot — Fruity, bright, bubbly
InstructionsPour 15ml Monin White Peach syrup into a flute. Top with chilled alcohol-free merlot and stir gently.
🍋 Caipiri-nah
Lime juice, sugar, crushed ice — Zesty, simple, Brazilian
InstructionsMuddle 2 lime wedges with 2 tsp sugar in a glass. Fill with crushed ice, top with soda water, and stir thoroughly.
☕ Espresso Marti-no
Espresso, vanilla extract, cocoa powder — Strong, sweet, smooth
InstructionsShake 50ml freshly brewed espresso, 1 drop vanilla extract and 1 tsp cocoa powder with ice for 10 seconds. Strain into a chilled martini glass and garnish with coffee beans.
🥂 Free & T
Tonic water, lime juice, elderflower cordial — Botanical, crisp, clean
InstructionsCombine 150ml tonic water, 10ml lime juice and 10ml elderflower cordial over ice. Stir gently and garnish with a lime wheel.
🍋 Margaritish
Lime juice, lemon juice, orange juice, maple syrup, tonic water — Citrusy, tangy, spritzy
InstructionsShake 20ml lime juice, 10ml lemon juice, 10ml orange juice and 10ml maple syrup with ice for 8 seconds. Strain into a salt-rimmed glass over ice and top with tonic water.
🍇 Sans-gria
Monin Sangria syrup, alcohol-free merlot, orange juice — Bright, floral, tangy
InstructionsCombine 50ml Monin Sangria syrup, 50ml alcohol-free merlot, and 50ml orange juice in a glass with ice. Stir gently and garnish with orange slices and a sprig of mint.
🥭 Popstar Marti-no
Mango juice, lime juice, Monin White Peach syrup, vanilla — Tropical, cheeky, fun
InstructionsShake 50ml mango juice, 15ml lime juice, 10ml Monin White Peach syrup and 1 drop vanilla extract with ice for 10 seconds. Strain into a coupe and serve with a side shot of sparkling water.
